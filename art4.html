The Bird Seller

Author: Vicente Manansala

This Cubist genre painting was created by Vicente Manansala Created in 1976, which depicts a Filipino vendor selling tropical birds. The scene is a commentary on everyday life, simple living, and the hardships faced by common people. It reflects the need for social justice and the recognition of the dignity of every individual, resonating with the goal of ensuring peace and justice
