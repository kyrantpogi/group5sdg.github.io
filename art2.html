La Colombe (Dove of Peace)

Author: Pablo Picasso

The “Dove of Peace” by Pablo Picasso, a simple yet deep artwork created in 1949 for the World Peace Congress in Paris, symbolizes hope and unity through its abstract representation of a white dove against a vibrant black background, conveying the message of peace, justice, and strong institutions amidst the turmoil of World War II, serving as an inspiring example of how art can promote positive change and inspire meaningful conversations.
